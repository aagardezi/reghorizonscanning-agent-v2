# Sanctions Auditing Guidelines

When auditing sanctions list matches and counterparty exposure:
- **Screening Rigor**: Match entities using full names, spelling variations, phonetic representations, and strong/weak aliases. Record the unique OFSI Group ID.
- **Regime Isolation**: Distinguish between UK (OFSI), US (OFAC), and UN regimes. Identify potential US nexus or extraterritorial overlaps.
- **Counterparty Mapping**: Map designated targets against all core business exposure vectors:
  * Policyholders (active cover, new business)
  * Beneficiaries & Claims Payees
  * Reinsurance Counterparties
  * Custodians, Asset Managers, and Investment Portfolios
- **Zero-Tolerance Principle**: Sanctions compliance is strict liability. Promptly flag any direct or indirect match for immediate legal escalation.
