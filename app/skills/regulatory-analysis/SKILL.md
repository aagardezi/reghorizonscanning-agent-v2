# Regulatory Analysis Methodology

When performing regulatory compliance assessments, apply the following systematic process:
- **Obligation Identification**: Extract the precise rule, constraint, or mandate from the source text.
- **Baseline Comparison**: Compare the new requirements with typical current industry practices.
- **Functional Mapping**: Analyze and categorize the operational impact into the following specific internal departments:
  * **Actuarial & Risk**: Capital modeling, reserves, solvency ratios.
  * **IT & Data Architecture**: Reporting systems, data pipelines, database schema.
  * **Compliance & Governance**: Policies, procedures, Board oversight, SMF attestations.
  * **Claims & Operations**: Customer-facing claim journeys, vulnerability support.
  * **Underwriting & Reinsurance**: Policy terms, pricing, counterparty due diligence.
- **Timelines & Status**: Extract deadlines and implementation phases, confirming status as live/active or pending.
